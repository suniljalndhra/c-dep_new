Mar 24,2023

Indian Ocean Rim Association for Regional Cooperation (IOR-ARC)

C-DEP was invited by the Indian Ocean Region Association of Regional Cooperation (IOR-ARC) to present the details of the eHealth Centre (eHC). The purpose of this was to consider the eHC adoption in the member countries from South Africa, Iran, India, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, to Madagascar, Mozambique, East Africa, Australia, and Indonesia among others.

Mar 17,2023

National Conclave on Technological Sovereignty 2013

The National Conclave on Technological Sovereignty, 2013, was held because it is believed that the world is now in an era where warfare will be asymmetric in nature and will be conducted using non-traditional means. The discussions on strategic implications of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) revolved around attaining technological sovereignty and indigenous development through combined partnership of public and private sector undertakings.
